What is your BMI


Find out now with this simple calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from a person's weight and height, used to categorize that can indicate whether they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.


Underweight: BMI less than 18.5


Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9


Overweight: BMI 25–29.9


Obesity: BMI 30 or greater
